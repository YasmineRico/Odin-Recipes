Description

This is a fairly simple yet satisfying quick meal sandwich. I typically have this for lunch breaks and honestly one is more than enough. I pair this sandwhich with some fruit and nuts on the side, it's the bomb dot com!



Ingredients

  • 2 bread slices
  • Pickle Slices
  • Your faveorite type of sliced cheese
  • Turkey slices or your favorite non-meat substitute
  • Sour Cream
  • Mustard
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Directions

  1. Make the sauce first: combine the mayo and mustard in a small mixing bowl until you get a light yellow. This sauce was introduced to me by Bobs Burgers famous burger: Chive my car.
  2. Spread the sauce over the slices of bread.
  3. Add pickles to the slices, I love pickles so I always have extra
  4. Add the lettuce, tomatoes and any other vegetables you enjoy with your sandwhiches
  5. Add your slice of cheese
  6. Put your choice of protein on top
  7. Close the sandwhich

Bon Appetite!
