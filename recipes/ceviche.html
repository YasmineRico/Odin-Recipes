My Famous Fish Ceviche Recipe

Description

Ceviche is popular Latin dish that is typically served as an appetizer (I make sure to make enough to last me a full day). Ceviche can be made with fish and/or shrimp. Some restaurants may serve the fish raw, however, I prefer cooked fish to stay on the safe side. For my recipe I prefer to eat cooked fish just to stay on the safe side.
